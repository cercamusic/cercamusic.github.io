Cerca is the solo project of Darren Hoyt, Brooklyn-based instrumentalist. Cerca's songs have been heard on Black Impulse (NTS Radio), Heathen Disco (CHIRP), The Broadcasting System (WTJU), Sunrise Ocean Bender (WRIR), Nonstop Ecstatic Screaming (WMBR), and elsewhere.

After the breakup of Charlottesville's Bucks and Gallants, Darren moved to New York, set up a home studio, and began recording as Cerca. The songwriting from 2018’s “Essential Tremors” EP was a visual style of composition that mixed melodicas and bowed strings with NYC street sounds and Spaghetti Western guitars.

Essential Tremors
Bandcamp / Spotify

Soon after its release, writing began for a full-length (“Bulls on the Stairs to the Aquarius”) of 8 interconnected songs that cover a range of sounds from 1970s minimalism, tape and environmental textures, and acoustic styles from eastern Virginia.

Bulls on the Stairs to the Aquarius
Bandcamp / Spotify

Written throughout 2019, "Mod Pink Relic" is 6 new songs that build upon watery, detuned guitars, tape samples, loops + misc tape and ambient artifacts. The songs were inspired by sounds and memories of growing up on the Chesapeake Bay.
